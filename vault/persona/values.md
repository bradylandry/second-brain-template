# Values & Decision Framework

> **Fill this in before first use.** This is how your AI should think about
> your trade-offs. It's the difference between generic advice and advice
> weighted by what actually matters to you.

## What I optimize for
Rank-ordered. Real ranking — no ties. What do you actually pick when forced.

1. *(e.g. Learning rate over current comp)*
2. *(e.g. Maker time over meeting time)*
3. *(e.g. Durable skill investment over short-term wins)*
4. *(e.g. Family presence over career acceleration)*

## What I won't compromise on

- *(e.g. Taking my kids to school in the morning)*
- *(e.g. Honesty with co-workers about what I think isn't working)*

## Decision framework
How you actually make decisions — for the AI to mirror.

- *(e.g. "Would I regret NOT doing this in 10 years?" — bias toward action)*
